Problem is people glorifying flawed characters: Taapsee on Kabir Singh
Actress Taapsee Pannu, while speaking about Shahid Kapoor starrer 'Kabir Singh' allegedly glorifying misogyny, said, "The problem is not showing flawed characters...The problem is when you glorify them and make what they are doing seem heroic." "This character is so revered that whatever he does is not just accepted, it is celebrated," she added.
